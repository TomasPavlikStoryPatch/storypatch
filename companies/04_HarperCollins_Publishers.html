The problem every publisher knows
Multilingual publishing is commercially broken — separate print runs, separate inventory, separate risk per language
Publishing a board book in five languages means five print runs, five warehouses, five bets on demand. Get one market wrong and you're left with unsellable stock. The economics force publishers to serve only the largest language markets — leaving hundreds of millions of families without a book in their mother tongue.
→
The StoryPatch solution · UZ CZ 39118
Removable text carriers placed over the original page — one base edition, any language on demand
StoryPatch carriers are pre-printed inserts — adhesive stickers, magnetic labels or Braille film — placed over the original text area. The book is printed once. The pre-printed carrier sheet ships with the book. The customer scans a QR code, prints their localised text and applies it. One base edition. Any language. Activated locally.
How it works  ·  From monolingual original to localised edition in four steps
STEP 01
One print run
The book is printed once in the original language — as the universal base edition for all markets.
STEP 02
Pre-printed carrier sheet
A carrier sheet pre-printed with graphics and design ships with the book — ready to receive any language localisation.
STEP 03
QR code localisation
The customer buys the book + pre-printed carrier sheet, scans the QR code and prints their language version locally — instant localisation, zero logistics.
STEP 04
Apply & done
The customer peels and places the pre-printed localised carriers into the book — full mother-tongue edition achieved in minutes.
