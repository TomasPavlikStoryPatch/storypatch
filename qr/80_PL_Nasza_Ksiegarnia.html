The current problem
Poland hosts 1–2M Ukrainian refugees — the largest humanitarian arrival in modern Polish history — yet no Polish publisher offers a single Ukrainian-language board book for ages 0–3. Nasza Księgarnia has the heritage, the titles and the distribution to be first.
Since 2022, Poland has absorbed 1–2M Ukrainian refugees — transforming the country overnight into one of Europe's most multilingual nations. German (148k), Kashubian (200k recognised minority) and Belarusian (48k) communities add further institutional demand. Poland's Education Act mandates language support in all żłobki and przedszkola. Not one of Poland's major children's publishers — including Nasza Księgarnia — has responded with a multilingual 0–3 product. As Poland's oldest and most trusted children's publisher, Nasza Księgarnia is the natural first-mover.

Pre-printed translation carriers on Nasza Księgarnia 0–3 titles.
