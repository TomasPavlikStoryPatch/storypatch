The current problem
Sami (co-official), Polish (110k), Somali (45k), Arabic — strong public library procurement and official indigenous language mandate
Norway has 900k+ residents with immigrant background and two legally co-official Sami languages. Polish (110k), Somali (45k), Arabic (50k), Dari (40k) — communities whose children attend barnehager with no mother-tongue board books. Norway's public procurement system for libraries and barnehager is among the most professional in the world.

Solution · UZ CZ 39118
Removable text carriers: one print run, every language served
Pre-printed translation carriers placed over the original pages. One Norwegian base edition; language sets for Northern Sami, Polish, Somali, Arabic and Dari are produced centrally — no inventory risk per language.
How it works  ·  From original to multilingual range in four steps
STEP 01
One print run
Printed once in Norwegian (Bokmål) — the base for all community and Sami language groups.
STEP 02
Language carriers
Sets for Sami, PL, SO, AR, FA… produced as inserts — fraction of a reprint.
STEP 03
Barnehage & bibliotek channel
Every barnehage, folkebibliotek and bookshop receives the right set on demand.
STEP 04
Nordic redistribution
Unsold stock redistributed within the Nordic region from one production run.
