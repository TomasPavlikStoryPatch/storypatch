Solution · UZ CZ 39118
Removable text carriers: one print run, every language served
Pre-printed translation carriers placed over the original pages. Publishers produce one Japanese base edition; language sets for Korean, Chinese, Portuguese and Tagalog are produced centrally at a fraction of a reprint cost.

STEP 02
Language carriers
Sets for KO, ZH, PT-BR, TL… produced as inserts — fraction of a reprint.
STEP 03
Hoikuen channel
Every hoikuen, toshokan and bookshop receives the right language set on demand.
STEP 04
Asia-Pacific redistribution
Unsold stock redistributed to Korea, Taiwan or Singapore from one run.
