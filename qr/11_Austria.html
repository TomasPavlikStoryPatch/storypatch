The current problem
Turkish (350k), Serbian/Croatian (400k), Romanian communities — Vienna's documented multilingual demand with no 0–3 board book solution
Austria has a foreign-born population of 1.7M. Turkish (350k), Serbian (200k), Croatian (200k), Romanian (130k), Polish (120k) — communities whose children attend Kindergärten with no mother-tongue board books. Austria's publishing market — Ueberreuter, G&G Verlag — shares the German-language space but has no multilingual 0–3 product.

Solution · UZ CZ 39118
Removable text carriers: one print run, every language served
Pre-printed translation carriers placed over the original pages. One German base edition; language sets for Turkish, Serbian/Croatian and Romanian are produced centrally — no inventory risk per language, redistributable across the entire DACH region.
