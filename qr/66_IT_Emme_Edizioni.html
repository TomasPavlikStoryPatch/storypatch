The current problem
Emme Edizioni publishes some of Italy's most beautiful 0–3 titles — yet Romanian (1.1M), Arabic (500k) and Albanian (440k) families in Italy are unserved. As an independent, Emme can be first to market.
Emme Edizioni is one of Italy's most critically acclaimed children's publishers, known for the quality and design of its 0–3 board book range. As part of the Il Castoro group, Emme has strong relationships with Italian independent bookshops, school libraries and the Nati per Leggere institutional network. Not one Emme 0–3 title is available in a multilingual format — yet Italy's 300k+ non-Italian-speaking children 0–3 represent an entirely unaddressed market that a fast-moving independent can own.

Solution · UZ CZ 39118
Removable text carriers: one print run, every language served
Pre-printed translation carriers placed over Emme Edizioni 0–3 titles. One Italian base; carrier sets for Romanian, Arabic, Albanian, Chinese and Ukrainian produced centrally — no inventory risk, deployable through Emme's independent bookshop and Nati per Leggere institutional channels.
