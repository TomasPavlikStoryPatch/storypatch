The current problem
Arabic (200k), Somali (100k), Finnish (200k) minority speakers — strong welfare state procurement with no multilingual 0–3 product on the market
Sweden has 2.5M residents with a foreign background. Arabic (200k), Somali (100k), Finnish (200k), Dari/Pashto (100k), Polish (100k) — communities whose children attend förskola with no mother-tongue board books. Sweden's Skollag mandates mother-tongue support and the welfare state's library purchasing system is one of the most reliable institutional channels in Europe.

Solution · UZ CZ 39118
Removable text carriers: one print run, every language served
Pre-printed translation carriers placed over the original pages. One Swedish base edition; language sets for Arabic, Somali, Finnish, Dari and Polish are produced centrally — no inventory risk per language.
How it works  ·  From original to multilingual range in four steps
