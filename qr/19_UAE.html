The current problem
90% expat population — South Asian, Arab and Western families with children in a high-purchasing-power market with no multilingual 0–3 offer
The UAE has a population that is 90% expatriate. South Asian (Hindi, Urdu, Tamil, Malayalam, Tagalog), Arab (Egyptian, Levantine, Moroccan) and Western communities — families whose children attend nurseries with no mother-tongue board books. The UAE's premium book market has high purchasing power but no multilingual 0–3 format.
→
Solution · UZ CZ 39118
Removable text carriers: one print run, every language served
Pre-printed translation carriers placed over the original pages. One Arabic or English base edition; language sets for Hindi, Urdu, Tamil, Malayalam, Tagalog and Filipino are produced centrally — no inventory risk per language.
How it works  ·  From original to multilingual range in four steps
STEP 01
One print run
Printed once in Arabic or English — the base for all expat community languages.
STEP 02
Language carriers
Sets for HI, UR, TA, ML, TL… produced as inserts — fraction of a reprint.
STEP 03
Nursery & bookshop channel
Every licensed nursery, Virgin Megastore and Kinokuniya UAE receives the right set.
STEP 04
GCC redistribution
Unsold stock redistributed within GCC from one production run.
